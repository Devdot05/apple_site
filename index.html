What is a computer?

A computer is an electronic machine or devised for performing calculation and controlling operation that can be expresed either in logical or numirical terms

computer can also be defined as an electronic device designed to accept data., perform prescribed speed, and display the results of these operations.

Characteristics of Computer

  2. Acuracy: Computer do not make mistake in so fast as they are acurately programmed and are not faultyin term of component
